# Progress And Charts Review

Load this file when reviewing progress bars, rings, gauges, charts, and numeric overlays.

- Verify compliance with `references/policies/progress-charts.md`.
- Verify that visible progress strips, bars, tracks, and scrollbars stay at `20px` thickness or below.
- Verify that a single numeric ratio is not duplicated as both ring and linear progress on the same surface.
- Verify that progress fills, strokes, chart lines, and chart curves use the required shadow and gradient treatment without clipping.
- Verify that ring or gauge center text stays limited to the number or number plus one short status title, that units render smaller than the main number, and that explanatory copy stays outside the graphic body.
